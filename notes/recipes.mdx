### [Enchilada Casserole](https://healthyrecipesblogs.com/enchilada-casserole/#recipe)

Sauce:
- [ ] 1 onion, minced
- [ ] few garlic cloves, minced
- [ ] salt & pepper to taste
- [ ] buncha chili powder
- [ ] some cumin
- [ ] 15 oz can tomato sauce
- [ ] 1/2 cup water

Casserole: 
- [ ] couple of chicken breasts, grilled and shredded
- [ ] 4 oz can of green chile peppers
- [ ] 2(+) cups of sharp cheddar

1. Cook the onion with some olive oil until softened, like 5-7 minutes. Add the garlic and rest of the spices and cook until fragrant, like 30 secs. Add the tomato sauce and water, and simmer until it's thickened a bit, like 5 minutes.
1. Combine the sauce, shredded chicken, peppers, and half the cheese in a bowl. Put that in a baking dish, sprinkle with the remaining cheese, and bake until all the cheese is melted.
