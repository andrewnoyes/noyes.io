- [Shell Tricks That Actually Make Life Easier](https://blog.hofstede.it/shell-tricks-that-actually-make-life-easier-and-save-your-sanity/) - The “Works (Almost) Everywhere” Club:
  - `CTRL + W`: delete the word before the cursor 
  - `CTRL + U`: cut everything from the cursor to the beginning of the line
  - `CTRL + Y`: to paste ("yank") contents into prompt
  - `CTRL + A`: jump to beginning of line
  - `CTRL + E`: jump to end of line
  - `ALT + B`: move backward one word
  - `ALT + F`: move forward one word
