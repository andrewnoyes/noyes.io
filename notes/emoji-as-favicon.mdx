---
title: Emoji as a favicon
created: 12/21/2024
updated: 04/26/2026
tags: html,web
---

Put the emoji inside an **svg** element and set that as the favicon (from [css-tricks](https://css-tricks.com/emoji-as-a-favicon/)): 

```html
<link 
  rel="icon" 
  href="data:image/svg+xml,
    <svg xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22 viewBox=%220 0 100 100%22>
      <text y=%22.9em%22 font-size=%2290%22>🤙</text>
    </svg>"
>
```
